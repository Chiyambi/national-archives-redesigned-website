About This Bibliography

Dewey Decimal Classification System

This bibliography is organized using the Dewey Decimal Classification (DDC) system, a widely recognized library classification system that organizes knowledge into 10 main categories, with further subdivisions.

The entries below represent Malawian publications and imprints covering the period 1991-1996, organized by subject matter and discipline. Click on any category to view the publications in that field.

About the National Archives

This bibliography is maintained by the National Archives of Malawi Library in Zomba. All publishers in Malawi are required by the Printed Publications Act to deposit copies of their publications with the National Archives, ensuring a comprehensive record of Malawi's published heritage.

For more information: Visit the Bibliography Preface for historical context and publication history.

About

The National Archives of Malawi preserves and provides access to the nation's documentary heritage, safeguarding records of historical, administrative, and cultural significance.
