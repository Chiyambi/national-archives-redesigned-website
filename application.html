Declaration

Terms and Conditions

1. Government Approval Requirement

I understand that I have been granted access to archives and records in the custody of the Archivist for Malawi, strictly on the condition that the final draft of any written materials I produce which contains information derived from any of these documents must be submitted to the Malawi Government for approval before publication.

2. Confidentiality and Restricted Disclosure

I understand that I must not divulge any information gained from official documents to any unauthorized person, orally or in writing, without the previous sanction of the Malawi Government. I understand that these provisions apply not only for the period during which I am allowed access to official documents but also afterwards.

3. Compliance with Rules and Regulations

I undertake to abide by the rules and regulations of the National Archives as outlined in the Search Room Guidelines.

4. Submission of Completed Work

I shall cause to be delivered, at my own expense, one copy of any work which may result from my research, to the Archivist for Malawi within two months of the date of completion.

About

The National Archives of Malawi preserves and provides access to the nation's documentary heritage, safeguarding records of historical, administrative, and cultural significance.
