Visitor Information

Before visiting the National Archives, please note:

  • Admission Required: Visitors must complete an application form before accessing the search room
  • Rules & Regulations: All visitors must comply with search room rules
  • Advance Notice: For research involving specific collections, please contact us in advance
  • Access Hours: Monday-Friday: 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM; Saturday: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Special Requests: Email us at archives@sdnp.org.mw for specialized inquiries

About

The National Archives of Malawi preserves and provides access to the nation's documentary heritage, safeguarding records of historical, administrative, and cultural significance.
