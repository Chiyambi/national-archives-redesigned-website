National Archives of Malawi — Safeguarding the Nation’s Memory

Welcome to the National Archives of Malawi

The National Archives of Malawi is the official custodian of the nation’s public and private records of enduring value. We preserve, organize, and make accessible records that document Malawi’s history, governance, culture, and heritage for present and future generations.

Search Room Guidelines

To ensure a productive and respectful research environment, all visitors to our Search Room must familiarize themselves with our guidelines and regulations.

About

The National Archives of Malawi preserves and provides access to the nation's documentary heritage, safeguarding records of historical, administrative, and cultural significance.
