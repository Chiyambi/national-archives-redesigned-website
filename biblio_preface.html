Bibliography Preface

Malawi National Bibliography Overview and Historical Context

The first National Bibliography covering the two Rhodesias and Nyasaland was published in 1963 under the title "List of Publications Deposited in the Library of The National Archives" and it recorded the publications received in 1961. After the dissolution of the Central African Federation, the first Malawi National Bibliography, listing Malawian imprints only, appeared in 1965 and 1966 in mimeograph form under the title "List of Publications Deposited in the Library of the National Archives." The bibliography has since 1967 been produced annually or in cumulations of two to three years.

The last edition of the Malawi National Bibliography was the issue of 1994 which carried the issues of 1984 - 1990. This edition lists Malawian imprints covering the period 1991 - 1996. Although the bibliography is not fully comprehensive in coverage, it is hoped that it will still be a useful bibliographic tool to some African librarians.

The compiler of the bibliography would like to thank all publishers who complied with the requirement of the Printed Publications Act by depositing a copy of their publications in the National Archives Library which made it possible for the bibliography to be published.

O. W. Ambali
Librarian

Legal Foundation

The Malawi National Bibliography is maintained under the authority of the Printed Publications Act, which requires publishers to deposit copies of their publications in the National Archives Library. This legal requirement ensures the comprehensive collection and preservation of Malawian imprints.

Key Provision

All publishers in Malawi must, within two months of publication, deliver a copy of every printed work to the Government Archivist. This requirement ensures that the National Archives maintains a complete record of the nation's published heritage.

For more information about the Printed Publications Act and related legislation, please see the Laws of Malawi section.

Purpose and Scope

The Malawi National Bibliography serves as an essential bibliographic tool for:

  • African Librarians — Providing access to Malawian publications and imprints
  • Researchers and Scholars — Documenting the intellectual output of the nation
  • Publishers and Authors — Recording and preserving published works
  • National Heritage — Maintaining a comprehensive record of Malawi's publications

Although the bibliography is not fully comprehensive in coverage, it remains a valuable resource for understanding Malawi's literary and publishing heritage.

About

The National Archives of Malawi preserves and provides access to the nation's documentary heritage, safeguarding records of historical, administrative, and cultural significance.
